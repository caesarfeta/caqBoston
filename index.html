What is Capoeira?

Capoeira is a Brazilian martial art, hidden in dance. It came to Brazil when slaves were brought from Africa during the mid 1500s. Under the watchful eye of the slave owner, capoeira appeared as a harmless dance. After the abolition of slavery, capoeira survived in the poorer neighborhoods (despite persecution from police) until becoming legalized in the 1930's. Today, capoeira is taught around the world, with each group incorporating its own blend of movements, music, and philosophy.

CAQ Boston

We train and play in a supportive environment that emphasizes a thoughtful, creative, and technically sophisticated approach to capoeira's proud history. To provide a well-rounded experience, most classes begin with music training, then movement training, and finally a roda (pronounced: "HO-duh"), which is where students play each other drawing on what they've learned.

Our Lineage

Mestre Ombrinho

The head instructor at the New York Capoeira Center is Mestre Ombrinho. With over 30 years practicing capoeira and over 24 years experience teaching, he is the first North American Capoeira Master. He is renowned for making the art of Capoeira accessible to people of all ages and abilities. He began studying Capoeira in 1981 with Mestre Acordeon and Mestre Jelon, two internationally-recognized masters who brought capoeira to the United States. He made his first trip to Brazil in 1985 where he trained with many legendary masters, young and old. Having trained many capoeira styles including Regional, which emphasizes fighting aspects, and Angola, which emphasizes a more thoughtful game, Mestre Ombrinho was drawn to the latter and formed Capoeira Angola Quintal NY.

Contra-Mestre Graveto

Contra-Mestre Graveto has studied and trained under Mestre Ombrinho, Mestre Nô, Contra-Mestre Perere for over 15 years and has dedicated himself to the continuation of Capoeira Angola and in enriching the lives of those whom it touches. In addition to leading a group in Ithaca, he provides intensive guidance to students around the country including CAQ Boston.
